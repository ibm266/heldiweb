## What we use today

heldi.co.uk currently sets **no advertising or analytics cookies**.

We use browser **local storage** (not cookies) for strictly functional
purposes only:

| Key | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| `heldi_cart_v1`, `heldi_cart_id` | Remembering what's in your basket | Until you clear it |

Strictly necessary storage like this does not require consent under PECR.

## Checkout

Checkout runs on Shopify. Shopify sets cookies that are strictly necessary to
operate the checkout (session, cart and security cookies). See Shopify's own
cookie notice for details.

## When this will change

If we add analytics (e.g. GA4) or marketing pixels, those set optional

## Managing storage

You can clear local storage and cookies in your browser settings at any time;
your basket will simply be forgotten.
