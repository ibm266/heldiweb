## How we measure, in plain words

By default we count visits **anonymously and without cookies**. Our analytics
tool (PostHog, hosted in the EU) receives events with no cross-visit
identifier, and we have switched off the storage of IP addresses. We use this
only to produce aggregate statistics about how the site is used, relying on
the statistical-purposes provision of the Privacy and Electronic
Communications Regulations as amended by the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025.

You can switch even that off. Use the
[analytics choices panel](./cookie-policy.md#analytics-choices) at the bottom
of this page; it applies immediately and is free.

If you choose **"Count me in"** on our banner, we additionally set analytics
storage so we can recognise your browser between visits, and we record
session replays (what you scrolled, tapped and hovered) with anything you
type hidden. This only ever happens after you say yes, and you can undo it in
the same panel.

We set **no advertising or marketing cookies**. If that ever changes, we will
ask first with a separate choice; your analytics answer will never be reused
for advertising.

## Storage we use

Browser **local storage and session storage** (not cookies) for functional
purposes:

| Key | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| `heldi_cart_v1`, `heldi_cart_id` | Remembering what's in your basket | Until you clear it |
| `heldi_gifting_method` | Remembering how a gifting discount was applied | Until you clear it |
| `heldi_gifting_popup_seen` | Showing the family-discount note once per visit | Until the tab closes |
| `heldi_consent_v1` | Remembering your analytics choice | Until you clear it |
| `heldi_touch_v1` | Remembering which link brought you here | Until the tab closes |

Strictly necessary storage like this does not require consent under PECR.

Only after you choose "Count me in":

| Key | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| `heldi_first_touch_v1` | Keeping which link first brought you here | Until you change your choice |
| `ph_*` (cookie and local storage) | PostHog analytics identifier and session state | Until you change your choice |

## Checkout

Checkout runs on Shopify. Shopify sets cookies that are strictly necessary to
operate the checkout (session, cart and security cookies). See Shopify's own
cookie notice for details.

## Managing storage

Change your analytics choice any time in the panel at the bottom of this
page. You can also clear local storage and cookies in your browser settings;
your basket and your saved choice will simply be forgotten.
