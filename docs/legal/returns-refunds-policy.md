## Changed your mind? (14-day cooling-off)

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 you may cancel your order up to
**14 days after the day you receive it**, without giving a reason — with one
important exception for food products:

> **Sealed food products can only be returned if the seal is unbroken.**
> Once a pouch or sachet is opened we cannot accept it back, for health and
> hygiene reasons. This is the "sealed goods" exception in regulation
> 28(3)(a).

To cancel: email `[hello@heldi.co.uk — TBC]` within the 14 days, then send
the unopened item(s) back to the address above within 14 days of telling us.
Please include your order number.

- You pay the return postage for change-of-mind returns.
- We refund the product price plus the original standard delivery charge
  within **14 days** of receiving the goods back (or proof of postage),
  using your original payment method.
- We may reduce the refund if the value of the goods has been diminished by
  handling beyond what is necessary to inspect them.
- Free gifts (jars, masala dabba) that came with a returned order must be
  returned with it, or we may deduct their advertised value from the refund.

## Damaged, faulty or wrong items

Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 our products must be of satisfactory
quality, fit for purpose and as described. If something arrives damaged,
spoiled, past its best-before date, or we sent the wrong thing:

1. Email `[hello@heldi.co.uk — TBC]` with your order number and a photo
   within 30 days of delivery.
2. We will replace it or refund it in full — your choice — and we cover the
   return postage. You do not need to return perishable or damaged food for
   the refund unless we ask.

## Non-delivery

If your order does not arrive within 10 working days of the dispatch email,
contact us. Once we have confirmed the loss with Royal Mail we will resend or
refund in full.

## How refunds are paid

Refunds go back to the original payment method via our payment processor
(Shopify Payments). Allow 5-10 working days for your bank to show the credit
after we process it.

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights.
