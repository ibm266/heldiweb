## How to use (required section, flexible copy)

The site needs **some** how-to-use area. Headlines, icons, step count, and wording are all up for redesign.

**Must communicate (substance, not exact words):**

1. **In the pot** — Stir into dal/curry/raita after cooking, while the dish is **cooling** (not boiling). One spoonful/tablespoon per serving context.
2. **At the table** — Optional: leave the jar out so each person adds their own.

**Do not imply:** shaking, blending, or drinking as a shake.

The live site's `#how` section and stir gallery are examples — not templates. A single FAQ answer, two icons, or a three-step timeline all work.

## Optional (not compliance-required)

These appear in `back-prompt-master.md` for **pack design** but are **not** required on the website:

- Kicker ("Healthy, but the way my nani says it.")
- Long explainer paragraph
- Claim icon row layout (high protein / lactose free / vegetarian)
- Protein serving marketing callout
- Pack-specific layout rules (zipper zone, nutrition-left/ingredients-right row)
- "HOW TO GET HELDI" exact heading

Product claims (vegetarian, lactose-free, protein content) may still appear in marketing if substantiated — they just aren't locked to back-prompt copy or layout.
