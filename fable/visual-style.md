# Heldi visual style guide

For Claude Design / Fable: use this when generating illustrations, social graphics, ads, or lifestyle imagery.

## Overall aesthetic

**Warm editorial DTC meets Indian household warmth.**

- Full serif typography, no sans-serif in brand graphics
- Bold ink borders and **hard sticker shadows** (offset, no blur)
- Marigold + ink blue + cream palette, warm, not neon
- Playful details: decorated elephants, double rules, dashed gold frames, menu-card layouts
- Interaction-forward feel even in static assets, cards, pills, ticker bands

Not: minimalist tech startup, gym supplement brutalism, or generic food photography with cold grading.

## Logo & wordmark

- **HELDI** wordmark: Rozha One, ink blue `#011246` (or gold on ink backgrounds)
- Slight forward lean / italic energy (packaging uses skew; web uses PNG wordmark)
- Often bracketed by thick horizontal ink rules (3–5px)
- File: `references/heldi-wordmark.png`

Do not redraw the wordmark unless explicitly asked, use supplied asset.

## Elephant motif

Decorated **Indian elephant line illustration**, ornamental, not cartoonish.

- Large transparent version flanks hero (mirrored left/right)
- Small version anchors final CTA corners
- Fill/stroke colour: ink blue `#011246`
- Style: detailed line art with traditional decorative patterns (blanket, headpiece)
- Files: `references/elephant-large-transparent.png`, `references/elephant-small.png`

Elephants are **brand jewellery**, not mascots with faces/personalities.

## Product (pouch)

- Marigold/gold flexible pouch
- Ink blue elephant illustration on front
- Packaging copy: "Same Recipes / Same Taste / More Protein"
- Pronunciation: `/hel-dee/, adj. how my nani says "healthy."`
- File: `references/pouch.png`, **colour source of truth**

Product shots: pouch on white or cream sticker card with 3px ink border + 8px offset shadow.

## Hero film visual language

Reference: `references/hero-video-poster.png`

- Hyper-realistic family dining table scene

- Ink blue decorated elephants cross the scene (animated in video)

## Menu card graphics

When illustrating "tonight's table" or meal plans:

- Card: 292–300px wide aesthetic, 3px ink border, 18px radius
- Alternating schemes:
  - **Cream card:** cream bg, gold shadow `6px 6px 0 rgba(237,163,29,0.9)`
  - **Marigold card:** gold bg, cream shadow `6px 6px 0 rgba(248,240,222,0.85)`
- Tags: `LUNCH` / `DINNER` in pill with 2px ink border
- Dish rows: name ··· dotted leader ··· gram count in pill
- Heldi-boosted dishes: gram pill fills gold (cream card) or ink (marigold card)
- Double-rule divider inside card (two 3px ink lines)
- Totals band at bottom: contrasting gold/ink strip with protein total in Rozha One

## Photography direction

### Do

- UK Desi home kitchens and dining tables
- Shared meals, hands serving, multiple bowls, thali energy
- Dal, curry, raita, rice, papad, chaat, recognisable dishes
- Warm colour grade aligned to marigold/cream palette
- Natural light or warm tungsten

### Don't

- Shaker bottles, blenders, gym settings
- Single isolated protein powder scoop on grey seamless
- Cold blue clinical grading
- Stock photos that read as generic "Indian restaurant" rather than home

## Illustration vs. photo

| Use illustration | Use photography |
|------------------|-----------------|
| Elephants, wordmark, icons | Pouch hero, food, family table |
| Menu cards, infographics | Jar product shot |
| Pattern fills, ticker graphics | Hero film scenes |

Line illustrations always ink blue on marigold or cream, never full-colour rainbow.

## UI components in marketing graphics

Safe to echo homepage UI:

- **Ticker band:** ink bg, gold uppercase spaced text, bullet separators `•`
- **Eyebrow:** terracotta or gold, uppercase, letter-spaced
- **Stat grid:** terracotta Rozha One number + brown Gelasio label, 2px top rule
- **Pill CTA:** ink fill, gold text, fully rounded
- **Double rule:** signature section break

## Aspect ratios

| Use | Ratio |
|-----|-------|
| Hero video / web hero | 16:9 |
| Social landscape | 16:9 or 1.91:1 |
| Instagram feed | 1:1 or 4:5 |
| Stories | 9:16 |
| Pouch product | ~2:3 (portrait pouch) |

## Accessibility

- Ink on cream/gold: sufficient contrast for body text
- Gold on ink: used for nav and ticker, large text only
- Avoid terracotta alone on marigold for small text
- Provide alt-text thinking: describe dish + Heldi context, not just "food photo"

## Anti-patterns checklist

Before finalising any graphic, reject if it has:

- [ ] Sans-serif type
- [ ] Black `#000` instead of ink `#011246`
- [ ] Soft Gaussian drop shadows
- [ ] Gym / shake / blender imagery
- [ ] Multiple pouch SKUs or flavour variants
- [ ] "Scoop" language
- [ ] Cold corporate blue unrelated to brand ink
